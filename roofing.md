# Example Scorecard: Roofing and Restoration

## Core Customer Decision

Should the homeowner repair, replace, file a claim, monitor, or seek deeper inspection?

## Authority Sentence

An inspection-first roofing company helps homeowners make roof decisions through evidence, documentation, and claim-verifiable roof files instead of sales pressure.

## Proof Assets

- Inspection documentation examples
- Roof condition photo sets
- Claim verifiability pages
- Team member authority pages
- Storm damage education pages
- Dataset/model/tool pages
- Review responses that mention clarity and documentation

## Common Weak Spots

- Finished roof photos without inspection context
- Generic “quality roofing” language
- Reviews that praise friendliness but not decision support
- Website pages that sell before explaining
- Platform descriptions that do not match
