# Source Map for Total Market Authority Scorecard™

This project translates public-facing concepts from Richard Nasser's manuscripts into a public scoring framework, methodology paper, and educational app.

## Source Books

1. Total Market Authority
2. Start Visible
3. Compete, Don't Complete
4. Visible or Vanished

## Core Extracted Concepts

### Total Market Authority

Used for the main definition, ecosystem logic, platform agreement, market verifiability, the website-as-anchor concept, and the five authority layers: entity clarity, role clarity, language ownership, proof alignment, and behavioral confirmation.

### Start Visible

Used for startup legibility, legal and factual clarity, public/private boundary, glossary terms, website brain language, and the 1000-point scorecard structure.

### Compete, Don't Complete

Used for core positioning: completion gets a business listed; competition gets it chosen. The book also supports the idea that local authority depends on trust, clarity, and being the safer answer.

### Visible or Vanished

Used for AI-era visibility framing, AEO language, authority assets, proof streams, platform agreement, and the 1000/1000 visibility scorecard concept.

## Public / Private Boundary

This repository publishes the public scorecard framework, educational dimensions, and example methodology.

It does not publish private consulting implementation sequences, internal routing rules, proprietary prompt libraries, advanced scoring formulas, client audit procedures, private link strategy, or market takeover sequencing.
