# Total Market Authority Scorecard™ Checklist Questions

These questions power the first version of the public scorecard app.

## Authority Sentence

- Can the business explain itself in one strong sentence?
- Does the sentence include the customer, decision, method, and risk?
- Would a real customer understand it?
- Would a team member repeat it accurately?

## Language Layer

- Has the business named its process, standard, or method?
- Is the language specific and defensible?
- Can the language be proven with real examples?
- Is the language used consistently across platforms?

## Google Business Profile

- Is the business description specific?
- Are categories and services accurate?
- Do photos show process, not just outcomes?
- Are posts calm, useful, and educational?
- Is Q&A filled with real customer concerns?
- Do review responses reinforce the authority role?

## Social Proof Stream

- Do social profiles match the authority sentence?
- Do posts show proof sequences?
- Are captions educational and specific?
- Are highlights or pinned posts organized by authority themes?
- Does the content show continuity and real work?

## Corroboration Platforms

- Do third-party profiles match the business identity?
- Are project examples explained with context?
- Are reviews and photos aligned with the business role?
- Does the platform profile qualify the right customer?

## Website Anchor

- Does the website reinforce the ecosystem?
- Are proof assets organized into pages?
- Are definitions, case studies, FAQs, and process pages clear?
- Does the site reduce uncertainty before asking for the sale?

## Owner / Founder Entity

- Is the owner or founder clearly connected to the business?
- Does the owner bio explain the standard or philosophy?
- Does the public identity support trust and expertise?
- Is the owner connected to books, articles, videos, talks, or public frameworks?

## Proof Target

- Has the business created recent proof assets?
- Has each proof asset been reused across multiple platforms?
- Has review language been audited?
- Has profile consistency been audited?
- Has authority language been audited?
