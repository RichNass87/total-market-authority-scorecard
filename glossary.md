# Glossary

## AEO
Answer Engine Optimization. The practice of making a business easier for search and AI systems to explain as the answer to a real question.

## Authority Asset
A public proof element such as a case study, review, photo, video, FAQ, book, owner bio, process page, or credential page.

## Authority Sentence
A one-sentence explanation of who the business helps, what decision it helps them make, how it helps, and what risk it reduces.

## Business Entity
The connected public pattern of business name, owner, location, category, services, proof, credentials, profiles, reviews, content, and behavior.

## Founder Entity
The public identity of the owner or founder when that person supports trust, expertise, standards, and philosophy.

## Market Verifiability
The standard that a business claim should be supported by public proof.

## Platform Agreement
When public profiles, website pages, reviews, proof assets, books, ads, and schema reinforce the same role.

## Proof Stream
A steady flow of public evidence that shows process, judgment, context, and outcomes.

## Total Market Authority
A state where the market, platforms, customers, and AI systems can explain the business the same way.

## Website Brain
A website that organizes identity, decisions, proof, definitions, and conversion instead of acting like a static brochure.
